## How Achievements Work

Achievements unlock automatically as you play. When you unlock an achievement, you'll see a toast notification at the top of the screen with a sound effect. All progress is saved automatically and carries over between play sessions.

## Tips for Achievement Hunters

### üéØ Easy Quick Wins
Start with these achievements first:
- FIRST BLOOD (kill 1 enemy)
- POWER COLLECTOR (collect 10 powerups)
- POINT_MAKER (10k score)
- COMBO MASTER (5x combo)

### üî• Medium Challenge
Work toward these next:
- BOSS SLAYER (beat first boss)
- KILLER_INSTINCT (100 kills)
- LEVEL_UP (reach level 5)
- HIGH_SCORER (50k score)
- UNTOUCHABLE (1 perfect level)

### üí™ Hard Mode
The ultimate challenges:
- COMBO_LEGEND (10x combo) - Requires perfect spacing
- FLAWLESS_VICTORY (5 perfect levels) - Master-level skill
- ACE_PILOT (level 25) - Endurance test
- SCORE_LEGEND (100k) - All skills combined
- MASS_DESTRUCTION (1000 kills) - Long-term grind

### üß† Strategy Tips

**For Combo Achievements:**
- Use spread shot or triple shot powerups
- Focus on grouped formations
- Don't let more than 1.5 seconds pass between kills

**For Flawless Runs:**
- Memorize enemy patterns
- Use shield powerups as insurance
- Play defensively early, aggressively late
- Focus on dodging over scoring

**For High Scores:**
- Maximize combos (up to 2x multiplier at 5x combo)
- Don't miss powerups (10% drop rate)
- Defeat bosses (1000 points each)
- Survive longer for more opportunities

**For Boss Achievements:**
- Learn the 4 attack patterns
- Stay near bottom of screen for safety
- Use rapid fire powerup for DPS
- Shield powerup for clutch saves
